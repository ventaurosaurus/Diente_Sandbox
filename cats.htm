10 Cute Cat Pictures That Help Us Get Through Monday

Kaelee Nelson
Content Manager - Pawlicy Advisor

It’s our favorite day of the week again… Not.

Like many of you, Mondays don’t thrill us.

To get us through this fursome day, we compiled cute cat pictures to look at whenever things get a little hairy.

1. For Starters...

This cat can’t believe it’s Monday. I mean, really?

Wasn’t it just Monday 7 days ago? Why so often?

We feel you Three.spoiled.kitties, we feel you.

2. A Fabulous Queen

We’d probably like Mondays a lot more if we looked like the ultra-fab catastic__tani pulling up to the office.

Those sunglasses are ultra-cute, and the matching scarf and background are giving us life.

BRB, we’re getting some major fashion inspo.

3. “Pretty Please, Can I Go Back to Sleep?”

If this cute kitten from yourbeautifulcat isn’t making you feel a little better about Monday, then we don’t know what will.

Between those huge, beautiful eyes and that precious, pink nose, this tired kitty couldn’t get any cuter if it tried.

4. Here’s How We Feel About Mondays...

Another tongue out photo, but with much more attitude from jimmycatoppolo. Or should we say catitude?

Jimmy just wants the world to know that Mondays are not okay, and he doesn’t acknowledge their existence. We’re ready to back you up on this Jimmy!

5. Mondays Scare Us, Too

We are overwhelmed with the desire to scoop these kittens up and tell them it’ll be okay. Mondays can be scary guys, but don’t worry!

They come around every week, but so do Fridays and weekends! The next few days get better, and then the pawty weekend will return.

Wait a second... maybe today isn't so bad after all?! Thanks for inspiring the motivational pep talk, planetcatz!

6. A Philosophical Approach to the First Day of the Week

Some cats and humans are content with just getting through Mondays.

However, bambithecalicocat has decided to take a look at Mondays from a philosophical angle. Why do they come around, and why are they so pawful?

We’re not entirely sure ourselves, but we promise to give Bambi an update when we figure it out.

7. Mesmerizing Eyes Make Mondays Go Away

We are getting absolutely lost in lilbowiecat’s stunning eyes.

It’s impossible to decide which color is more beautiful, but if it gives us an excuse to stare at this picture and ignore Monday more and we are all for it!

8. Friends Make Life Better

Lifeofblueoscar and his bestie have the right idea. When things get rough, grab a friend and sit it out together.

Good company can make anything seem a little bit better.

9. We’ll Just Pretend It’s Sunday, Too

This smart cookie has decided it’s still Sunday and that they’re going to ignore Monday’s arrival entirely.

We definitely wish we could join them in heading back to bed for some much-needed Zzzs. Enjoy your nap for us, didi_meimei_0305!

10. Out of Office

When all else fails, thinking about more exciting things always helps us out.

This cute Bengal cat is already thinking about his next destination. So, where do you guys think he should go next?

We absolutely adore this pawsome pic. Thanks for sharing ikoandhawk!

Whether you embrace optimism or grumpiness, Monday is here. We hope these cute cat pictures help you get through your Monday!

We’re definitely feeling a little more pawsitive after looking at all of these cute kitties.

Share with another fur-loving friend for double the excitement and don't forget to follow @PawlicyAdvisor for more cat content!
